Company Policy Guidelines

1. Data Protection and Privacy
- All contracts must include GDPR compliance clauses
- Personal data handling must be explicitly defined
- Data breach notification procedures must be included

2. Liability and Indemnification
- Maximum liability cap should not exceed contract value
- Mutual indemnification clauses are required
- Force majeure clauses must be clearly defined

3. Payment Terms
- Payment terms should not exceed 60 days
- Late payment penalties must be reasonable
- Currency and payment method must be specified

4. Termination
- Notice period should be minimum 30 days
- Termination for convenience should be mutual
- Clear exit criteria must be defined

5. Intellectual Property
- IP ownership must be clearly defined
- License terms must be explicit
- Third-party IP must be disclosed
