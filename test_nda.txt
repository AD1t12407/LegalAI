MUTUAL NON-DISCLOSURE AGREEMENT

This Non-Disclosure Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into as of [DATE] between:

PARTY A: [Company A] ("Company A")
PARTY B: [Company B] ("Company B")

1. DEFINITION OF CONFIDENTIAL INFORMATION
1.1 "Confidential Information" means any proprietary or secret information disclosed by either party to the other party, including but not limited to:
    - Trade secrets
    - Technical information
    - Business plans
    - Customer data
    - Financial information
1.2 Information shall not be considered Confidential Information if it:
    - Is or becomes publicly available through no fault of the receiving party
    - Was known to the receiving party prior to disclosure
    - Is independently developed by the receiving party
    - Is disclosed with the written approval of the disclosing party

2. OBLIGATIONS OF RECEIVING PARTY
2.1 The receiving party shall:
    - Maintain the confidentiality of the information
    - Use the information only for the business purpose
    - Limit access to employees who need to know
    - Not reverse engineer any technical information
2.2 The receiving party shall protect the information with the same degree of care used to protect its own confidential information.

3. TERM AND TERMINATION
3.1 This Agreement shall remain in effect for 2 years from the date of execution.
3.2 The confidentiality obligations shall survive for 3 years after termination.
3.3 Upon termination or request, the receiving party shall:
    - Return all confidential materials
    - Destroy all copies and notes
    - Certify compliance in writing

4. REMEDIES
4.1 Both parties acknowledge that monetary damages may be inadequate for a breach.
4.2 The disclosing party shall be entitled to injunctive relief.
4.3 The prevailing party in any legal action shall be entitled to recover reasonable attorney fees.

5. GOVERNING LAW
5.1 This Agreement shall be governed by the laws of New York.
5.2 Any disputes shall be resolved in the courts of New York City.

6. MISCELLANEOUS
6.1 This Agreement represents the entire understanding between the parties.
6.2 Any modifications must be in writing and signed by both parties.
6.3 If any provision is found invalid, the remainder shall continue in effect.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, the parties have executed this Agreement as of the date first above written.
