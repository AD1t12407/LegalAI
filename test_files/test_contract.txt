SOFTWARE DEVELOPMENT AGREEMENT

This Software Development Agreement (the "Agreement") is made on May 24, 2024, between:

The Developer agrees to develop a custom enterprise software solution ("Software") as specified in Attachment A.

2. PAYMENT TERMS

7.1 Either party may terminate with 60 days written notice.
